Pathology tests
Graduates will be able to provide clinical indications and interpretation of results for the following tests.

    Arterial blood gases
    B-type natriuretic peptide
    Serum electrolytes
    2 hour glucose tolerance test
    Haemoglobin A1C
    Urinary microalbumin
    Serum creatinine
    Estimated Glomerular Filtration Rate
    Serum lipids and HDL cholesterol
    Liver function tests (Bilirubin, ALP, AST, GGT, ALT, Albumin, Protein)
    Hepatitis serology
    Creatine Kinase
    Serum amylase
    Thyroid Function Tests (TSH, free T4, free T3)
    Mg, Calcium, total, corrected and ionised
    25-OH Vit D, PTH
    Beta-HCG
    Example of drug detection / monitoring eg cotinine
    Example of sensitivity / monitoring eg tobramycin
    Example of toxic drug level detection eg paracetamol
    Full blood count: RBC indices, neutropoenia, neutrophyllia, thrombopoenia, thrombocytosis
    Ferritin
    Coag screen (INR, PTT)
    d-dimer
    EPG/IEPG
    HIV
    c-reactive protein
    PSA
    Faecal occult blood
    Histopathology as a component of staging of melanoma, Ca lung, Ca cervix, Ca colon, Ca breast and Ca prostate
    Bone marrow aspirate
